Welcome to Good Citizen

About The App

Good Citizen is an app that helps people hold others accountable for their bad behaviour in society.


Good Citizen can be used for reporting accidents, fights and quarrels, shootings, bad driving, open urination and/or defecation, littering, and any and all other behaviours and activities that are harmful to the society and to the wellbeing of others.
